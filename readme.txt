Shark Adventure -- Ashley DelVentura

- The player is the gray rectangle representing a shark.
- Move around the screen using the arrow keys to eat the blue "fish."
- You can only eat fish that are your size or smaller.
- Eat a fish by colliding with it.
- Avoid fish that are bigger than you.
- Player's remaining lives are shown at the top left (start with 3).
- Colliding with a fish that is too big to eat will cost you a life.
- Lose three lives and your game is over.
- Eating a smaller fish will restore one of your lives (maxed at 3 lives).
- As you eat smaller fish, your score will increase and shark will increase
  in size.
- Win the game by achieving a score of 50.
